# Veloria — The Underground Empire

Veloria is a civilization built entirely underground, carved into a vast cave system beneath the Ashfen Mountains. The total inhabited area spans 620,000 square kilometers across three connected cave networks called Tiers. The population is 8.4 million.

## The Three Tiers

The First Tier is the deepest layer, located 2,400 meters below the surface. It is the oldest inhabited zone and contains the imperial capital Draloch. The First Tier maintains a constant temperature of 34 degrees Celsius due to geothermal activity. Its population is 3.1 million.

The Second Tier sits at 1,200 meters depth and serves as the agricultural and manufacturing zone. It houses 4.2 million people and is lit artificially by a network of bioluminescent fungal panels called Glowfields. Each Glowfield panel covers 400 square meters and has a lifespan of 18 months before requiring replacement.

The Third Tier is the shallowest zone at 300 meters depth and functions as the trade and transit layer. It connects to the surface through 14 sealed tunnels called Vents. Only 1.1 million people live permanently in the Third Tier, but it processes 90 percent of Veloria's external trade.

## Draloch — The Imperial Capital

Draloch is carved from a single natural cavern that measures 12 kilometers in diameter and 800 meters in height. The city's population is 1.8 million. It is governed by the Obsidian Court, a body of 12 hereditary nobles called Wardens. The current head of the Obsidian Court is Warden-Prime Isara Denn, who has held the position for 23 years. Draloch is the only city in Veloria with natural geothermal vents that provide light as well as heat.

## The Ashfen Sickness

Thirty years ago, a fungal disease called the Ashfen Sickness spread through the Second Tier's Glowfield network. It destroyed 40 percent of all panels within six months, plunging large sections of the Second Tier into darkness. The crop failures that followed killed 190,000 people. The sickness was eventually contained by Mycologist Toven Ral, who developed a spore-resistant coating still applied to all new panels today. Toven Ral later became the founding director of the Veloria Institute of Fungal Sciences.

## The Veloria Institute of Fungal Sciences

The Veloria Institute of Fungal Sciences, called the VIFS, was established 28 years ago in the Second Tier. It trains 800 students per year. Every chief Glowfield engineer in Veloria's history has graduated from the VIFS. The current director is Professor Kael Morn, who studied under Toven Ral before Ral's death twelve years ago. Morn's own research focuses on extending Glowfield panel lifespan beyond 18 months through genetic modification of the fungal strains.

## The Vents

The 14 Vents connecting Veloria to the surface are each controlled by a Vent Authority appointed by the Obsidian Court. Each Vent is 6 meters in diameter and equipped with a pressure seal that can be closed in 4 seconds. The pressure seals were installed 15 years ago following the Grey Incursion, when surface raiders entered through Vent Seven and reached the Second Tier before being stopped. Following the Grey Incursion, all Vent Authorities were required to maintain a garrison of 200 soldiers.
